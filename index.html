Urban Chickens Home Page



Welcome to our site on urban chickens!
Here, you can learn the pro's and cons to having urban chickens, and decide if you can raise them yourself!





For the past few years, raising chickens in the backyard has become all the rage with families and single people alike.
According to LA Times, about 1% of American households have a chicken coop. However, there is a growing misconception that chickens are easy to care for,
when raising them is actually more complex than one would think. Of course, with this great responsibility also comes great benefits.
Sadly, chicken keeping is not for everybody, but we can help you decide today whether or not it is for you!
Urban chickens may be hard to take care of because they take your resources, can be gross, and are hard to protect,
but they make great pets because of their quality eggs, familial benefits, and the self-sufficiency they bring to your life.
